WonderWorks prides itself in its 100 exhibits that inspire kids’ imagination. For adults, it is a cluster of random things you never thought you needed to see. The below picture of the lobby will already give you an idea of what it's like.

Familiarity

There are many recognizable concepts like FedEx and Mona Lisa. This interactive museum, however, subverts our expectations of them.

Mass Production

There are so many locations that are nearly identical to each other. Much of their exhibits are also very similar, such as this wall that lets you see the outline of your body.
